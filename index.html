About

I am a 4th-year undergraduate student at Kangwon National University, advised by Dohyung Kim. My research focuses on Efficient AI, bridging the gap between heavy AI architectures and real-time execution. (1) Optimizing existing processes using AI to enhance efficiency, and (2) Accelerating and slimming down heavy AI models (e.g., LLMs) for efficient inference and training.
